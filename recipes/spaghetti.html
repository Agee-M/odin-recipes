Spaghetti


The 2nd Best Dish There Is

The 1st being more spaghetti.

This recipe will focus on a simple, yet mouthwatering home favorite.
You can never get enough!

Ingredients

  • 1 box of spaghetti noodles
  • A 16oz jar of your favorite sauce (we like Rao's Marinara)
  • 1lb of ground beef
  • 1/2 cup of onion minced
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Set a bot of water on the stove with high heat - Get it to boil
  2. Cook ground beef and onion in a large pot over medium until browned
  3. Drain fat and poor in your favorite sauce
  4. Cover and simmer occasionally until it's at a thicker consistency
  5. Salt to taste
  6. While the sauce is simmering, place the noodles into the boiling pot of water for 7 minutes (or follow box instructions)
  7. Remove and drain the noodles after they're done, mixing it in with the pot of sauce
  8. Serve the plate of delicious and enjoy!
