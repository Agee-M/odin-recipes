Homemade Oats


WARNING! DELICIOUS OATS!

Are you sick and tired of bland mush?
Do you need a recipe that'll make something more than inedible clay?
Are oats your only path to salvation?
Then look no further.

You were made for this recipe.

Ingredients

  • A half cup of old-fashioned oats
  • 2 tsp of brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp of your favorite peanut butter
  • A half cup of milk
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Poor your milk and oats into a bowl
    • Your goal is to get the milk to cover the oats, but not make a soup
  2. Add in all of your other ingredients except for salt
  3. Plop the bowl into a microwave on top of a plate
  4. Cook for about 3 minutes
  5. Make sure you have enough milk that it won't dry up completely
    • Go for porridge-like consistency.
    • It's always better to add milk after than to needing to reduce milk further.
  6. Mix well and enjoy!

    Optional:

    • You can add oats to your oats. Specifically, granola!
    • You can add a drizzle of dark maple syrup on top
      • The flavor is to die for!
    • Choose a peanut butter so good you can eat the jar alone!
    • You can chop up some fresh fruit to place on top
