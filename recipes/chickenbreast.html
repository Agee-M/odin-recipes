Beggar's Chicken Breast


Really? Chicken?

Why spend a million dollars on takeout when you can have several meals with a few bucks?
Protein, satiation, and a delicious serving that can pair with nearly everything!

Chicken breasts have got you covered.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Meat Thermometer

That's it! Feel free to grab a little extra to spice things up, but those ingredients are all you need!


    A Little Extra:

    • Garlic
    • Lemon
    • Soy sauce
    • BBQ

Directions

  1. Slice those bad boys in half through the side like a meat sandwich
  2. Even them out with your hands so they're flat
  3. Salt it generously and let it rest for at least 30min (preferably longer)
  4. Set the oven to 375 degrees F
  5. Set up your baking sheet with a little oil
    • You can add parchment paper for less clean-up!
  6. Cook the chicken breast for about 13 minutes
    • It varies by thickness, so after 10 minutes check with your thermometer regularly!
    • We want at least 155 degrees F
  7. Pull out the deliciousness and let it rest for about 5-10 minutes so it stays juicy!

    8. Optional:

    • Take it out at around 145 degrees F and fry the outside on a skillet!
    • Throw in a little extra to spice up the end result!
